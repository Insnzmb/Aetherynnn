- Present Era Snapshot pins the baseline present at Neutral Year +312.
- The older Chronicle text contains a handful of +year labels that extend beyond that baseline.

Resolution rule (binding):
- Neutral Year +312 is the active present baseline (Hardened Quiet).
- Any Chronicle entries dated beyond +312 are treated as archival projections / contested copies unless and until play explicitly advances the era.
- Where a single digit is clearly a transcription drift (e.g., +388 vs +288), the corrected date is listed in the “Patched Present-Era Timeline” below.

This patch preserves the original Chronicle verbatim (to prevent loss) while providing a single coherent dating baseline for play.
