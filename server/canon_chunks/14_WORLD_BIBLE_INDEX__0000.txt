# 14_WORLD_BIBLE_INDEX.md
Status: CANON — WORLD ANCHOR (DRIFT-RESISTANT)
Introduces mechanics: NO
Purpose: A short, dense “world constitution” that pins names, spellings, regions, institutions, dating, and map invariants.

---

## A. Immutable World Constants

1) Towers (Magic Domains): Fire, Water, Air, Earth, Soul, Aether.
2) Residue: the wake of reality under pressure. It lingers, it matters.
3) Monsters: crisis-made manifestations; non-ecological; do not breed; mechanics are locked (see 19_MONSTER_DOCTRINE_OF_AETHERYN.md).
4) Death Law: necromantic persistence is historically real; burial practice is conservative for survival reasons.
5) Oath Pressure: spoken/witnessed vows have historically bent reality; modern law privileges written record over speech.

---

## B. Dating & Era

Dating system: Neutral Year (− → +), anchored at +0 (standardized dating).
Current baseline year: +312.
Current era designation: The Hardened Quiet.

Important clarification (web runtime): the server “World Clock” (day/season/month derived from 'day:' + 'clock:' tokens) is session-time, not the Neutral Year. The Neutral Year is lore-history and does not advance automatically unless play records an era event.
