- The playable landmass is approximately **U.S.-scale**.

## REGION PROFILES (Macro-Regions)

### Veythar Empire
Veythar runs on record-keeping as a form of weather control. The empire believes disorder is a kind of contagious residue: invisible until it isn’t.

Governance: layered offices, overlapping mandates, and “temporary” emergency authorities that never quite expire.
Magic posture: permissive only when logged; suspicion is procedural, not personal.
Everyday texture: ink-stained fingertips, stamped travel slips, watchmen with ledger satchels.
Trade: iron tools, shock-ward fittings, paper, lamp oil.
Common taboo: unregistered miracles in public spaces.

Speech + naming: clipped official phrasing; surnames often occupational or place-tied (“of Ironspire,” “Ward-Drake,” “Sealwright”).
