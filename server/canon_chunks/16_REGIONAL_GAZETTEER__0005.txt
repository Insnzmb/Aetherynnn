### Frozen Reaches
The Frozen Reaches run on consequence, not comfort. The land is beautiful in the way a blade is beautiful.

Governance: wardens and survival councils; law is mostly burial law and ration law.
Everyday texture: smoke houses, bone markers, cold-ward charms, short funerals.
Trade: furs, preserved meat, iceglass, cold-ward craft.

Speech + naming: harsh consonants, clipped phrases. Places often include frost-, ash-, wind-, hold.

### Shattered Marches
The Marches are where history broke and never fully cleaned up. People live there anyway, because someone always lives where the money is dangerous.

Governance: salvage assemblies, ruin-claims, and “ownership by survival.”
Magic posture: pragmatic. Everyone knows residue; everyone has scars.
Everyday texture: scavenger tags, ash-lines on doorframes, improvised ward nails, markets selling items nobody admits to needing.
Trade: salvage, relic-adjacent scrap, ward components.

Speech + naming: slang-heavy; places favor material metaphors (“Blackglass,” “Riftshadow,” “Cinder”).
