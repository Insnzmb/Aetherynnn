#### PREDATORS & SCAVENGERS

##### ICE-SCAVEN LURKER
**Trophic Role:** Scavenger / Ambush Carnivore
**Population:** Rare

Pale, translucent-bodied creature with internal organs faintly visible.
Metabolism slows nearly to stillness.

**Diet (typical):**
Frozen carcasses; opportunistic strikes on warm-blooded animals near remains.

**Behavior:**
Waits weeks beside frozen carcasses.
Strikes only when heat is detected at close range.

**Human Risk (extremely rare):**
Attacks happen when people linger at a carcass site (salvage, burial, butchery), sleep too near the dead, or bleed onto snow.

**Human Interaction:**
Considered a bad omen rather than a threat.
Bodies are burned quickly to deny it food.

**Environmental Signs:**
Unexplained preservation of remains, frost patterns disturbed.

#### FOOD-WEB NOTES (Cold)
- **Ice-Scaven Lurker →** carcass sites, weak/bleeding animals, occasional opportunistic bite-and-drag
