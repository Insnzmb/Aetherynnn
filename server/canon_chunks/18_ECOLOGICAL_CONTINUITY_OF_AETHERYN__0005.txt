#### PREDATORS & SCAVENGERS

##### RIVER GLASSER
**Trophic Role:** Semi-Aquatic Predator
**Population:** Uncommon

Flat-bodied swimmer with reflective scale clusters that scatter light.
Almost invisible beneath moving water.

**Diet (typical):**
Fish, amphibians, and small mammals; larger prey taken at crossings.

**Behavior:**
Consumes fish and small mammals.
Avoids boats; attacks wading creatures.

**Human Risk (extremely rare):**
Usually avoids groups and boats. Attacks occur when a lone person wades at dusk/dawn, during flood muddiness, or when baited by blood from a recent kill.

**Human Interaction:**
Fisherfolk wear dull-cloth leggings.
Glasser oil is valuable for lamps.

**Environmental Signs:**
Blinded fish, rippling light distortions.

#### FOOD-WEB NOTES (Wetlands)
- **River Glasser →** fish, amphibians, small mammals, occasional Thornback calves at crossings
