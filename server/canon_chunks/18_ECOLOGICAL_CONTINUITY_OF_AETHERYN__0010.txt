#### THE ASHEN WITNESS
*(Often named for the event it arrives after: “The Smoke Judge,” “The Gallows-Glow,” “After the Census Burned.”)*

**Type:** Crisis Observer / Execution Echo
**Threat:** High
**HP:** 115
**DR:** 3
**Magic Resistance:** Medium
**Move:** Quick; short blink-steps through smoke/ash within sight
**Senses:** Sees violence, broken testimony, and unburied dead

**Description:**
A human silhouette burned hollow, interior lit by ember-glow. Faces surface briefly in its trailing smoke—eyes open, mouths shaping words that never land.

**Behavior:**
It appears after executions, massacres, purges, or oath-trials that ended in blood. It does not strike first unless attacked or lied to. It “tests” groups by forcing moments of truth—silences that won’t hold, confessions that slip out sideways.
