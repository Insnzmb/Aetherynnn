#### THE VOW-SHEAR
*(Named for what it ruins: “The Marriage-Knife,” “The Treaty-Threader,” “The Oath-Splitter.”)*

**Type:** Crisis Stalker / Oath-Pressure Predator
**Threat:** Extreme
**HP:** 165
**DR:** 4
**Magic Resistance:** High
**Move:** Very fast in straight lines; struggles to turn
**Senses:** Detects sworn bonds and spoken contracts

**Description:**
A blade-thin quadruped made of braided cord and bone, its joints tied in ceremonial knots. Where it steps, fibers fray. Voices nearby feel “loose,” like words don’t grip.

**Behavior:**
Drawn to crowded oaths: temples, courts, contracts, guild pledges, marriage rites, conscription lines. It strikes when people are already under pressure—when a promise is being forced, not chosen.

**Combat:**
- **Shear Lunge:** Piercing hit; on a strong strike, it “cuts” a spoken plan—separating allies (forced reposition, broken formation).
- **Knot-Whip:** Mid-range lash that can disarm or trip.
- **Unbind (1/scene):** Cancels one ongoing magical effect that relies on commitment/continuity (wards, tethers, bindings).
- **Fray Aura (Passive):** Coordination is harder in its presence: shouted orders mishear, allies hesitate.

**Defeat:**
Killable, but best handled with **tight formation, nets, and cornering**. It hates cramped spaces where it can’t build line speed.

**Signs:**
Knots unraveling overnight. Contracts splitting at the fold. Wedding cords found frayed as if chewed.
