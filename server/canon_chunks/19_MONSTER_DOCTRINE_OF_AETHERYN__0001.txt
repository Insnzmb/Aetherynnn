## Universal Expression Tag — [Volatile] (High Crisis)

At **Crisis 6+**, monsters commonly manifest as **Ruptured**: they gain **[Volatile]**.

**[Volatile]:** When the monster dies, its body ruptures into unstable residue. The GM expresses this as a short-range hazard (default: **d6** harm/effect in an adjacent zone or immediate radius, using the active engine’s hazard conventions).

This does not change the monster’s core statline; it changes what the rupture does to the scene.

# V. BEHAVIORAL INTELLIGENCE LAYER

Monsters prioritize:
- Densest energy clusters
- Visible authority
- Open wounds (physical or institutional)

Each archetype has tactical personality:
- Skirmisher tests perimeter
- Juggernaut fixates on obstacles
- Hunter isolates prey
- Controller shapes terrain
- Executioner waits for vulnerability
- Crisis Avatar forces phase shifts
