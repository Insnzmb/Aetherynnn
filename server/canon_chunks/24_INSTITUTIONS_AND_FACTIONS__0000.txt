## MAJOR INSTITUTIONAL BLOCS

### Veythar Imperial Residuum Office
Function: Centralized tracking of magical residue and unauthorized casting.
Scope: Urban centers and trade corridors.
Behavior: Surveillance-forward; documentation heavy; rarely overt.

### Eldrion Sun-Court
Function: Ritual legitimacy and agricultural sanctification.
Scope: Noble houses and seasonal rites.
Behavior: Public ceremony masking private political volatility.

### Rhenwall Dock Consortium
Function: Maritime trade arbitration and galvanic infrastructure.
Scope: Inter-city commerce and port security.
Behavior: Profit-first; loyalty transactional.

### Sapphire Dominion Registry Authority
Function: Bureaucratic consolidation and infrastructure reinforcement.
Scope: Records, permits, guild certifications.
Behavior: Procedural dominance; slow but relentless.

### Southern Sands Caravan Compact
Function: Route protection and desert logistics.
Scope: Caravan guilds
