# 26_SCARS_AND_STARTER_HOOKS.md
Status: CANON — WORLD GRAVITY (PLAY SEEDS)
Introduces mechanics: NO
Purpose: Bind history to geography and give the GM stable “first quests” without railroading.

---

## A. Canonical Scars (World-Scale)

### 1) The Black Orchard (−1666, sealed)
Truth: a miracle-land fed thousands; then the dead sprouted with the crops. The land was sealed and erased.
Modern consequence: quarantine doctrine and “burn-before-bloom” paranoia in agrarian regions.

### 2) The Ash Census (−1987)
Truth: regions began counting magic use. Obscurity ended.
Modern consequence: licenses, registries, guild charters; uncounted casting is treated as structural threat.

### 3) The Burning of Hesh (−972)
Truth: a false resurrection was exposed; the city burned itself.
Modern consequence: resurrection taboos and burial compliance rituals.

### 4) The War of Seven Witnesses (−1237)
Truth: incompatible divine testimony; states chose witnesses and went to war.
Modern consequence: skepticism toward prophets; institutionalized containment of “public miracles.”

### 5) The Ledger Fire (+88)
Truth: a provincial archive burned; debts vanished, grudges did not.
Modern consequence: registry hardening; fear of record loss; rumor economies.

### 6) The Three-Day Quiet (−412)
Truth: all birds fell silent across three regions.
Modern consequence: omen folklore that still shapes travel behavior.
