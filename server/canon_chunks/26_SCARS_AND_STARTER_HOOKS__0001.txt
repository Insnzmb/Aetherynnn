## C. Starter Nudge Quests (Non-Railroad)
Each hook is a nudge, not a mandate. Use whichever matches the player’s starting location.

### Veythar / Paper-Law Hooks
1) A travel permit is misfiled under the PC’s name; an inspector arrives with the wrong confidence.
2) A courier offers coin to carry a sealed packet that should not exist.
3) A registry clerk begs the PC to retrieve a ledger page before it gets “corrected.”

### Eldrion / Harvest-Rite Hooks
1) Orchard bees vanish overnight; the Sun-Court wants a quiet explanation.
2) A wedding oath is delayed “for safety.” The couple asks for help.
3) A shrine’s wax seals are found softened without heat.

### Rhenwall / Dock-Hub Hooks
1) A ship refuses to dock because the harbor lights “blink wrong.”
2) A tariff marker changes between morning and noon.
3) A dockworker claims a crate “weighs like a name.”

### Sapphire / Registry Hooks
1) A bridge permit suddenly requires an impossible ancestor.
2) A ward lattice hums at night and the inspectors won’t say why.
3) A missing stamp makes a person legally dead.

### Southern Sands / Route Hooks
1) A well-right marker is moved; a caravan is about to start a war over it.
2) A route token appears in the PC’s pack with no memory of where it came from.
3) A sandstorm reveals a caravan that died last week—still walking.

### Frozen Reaches / Burial Hooks
1) A body will not stay burned; the wardens want witnesses, not rumors.
2) A cold-ward charm cracks in a pattern older than the village.
3) Something drags graves open without taking the dead.
