# II. THE WARRIOR --- RELIC ASCENSION

The Warrior survived the god-killing.

Due to divine overload, the Warrior crystallized into a Relic-class
entity.

## Relic Classification

Tier IV --- World-Shaping (per Relic Doctrine)

## Fragmentation

The relic fragmented into conceptual anchors representing:

-   Discipline
-   Precision
-   Resolve
-   Endurance

Fragments remain dormant individually.

## Activation Requirements

Full activation requires:

-   One Mage
-   One Warrior
-   Voluntary cooperation
-   Divine pressure spike present
-   Fragment convergence

## Effect

One declared action resolves at maximum allowable narrative margin
within engine constraints.

This does NOT: - Rewrite past events - Modify dice size - Override
canonical locks

It creates a decisive forward canon event.

## Cost

-   Regional instability spike
-   Increased monster manifestation probability
-   Both participants permanently altered
-   Potential amplification of wandering rupture

Tone: The world holds its breath. The act carries sorrow weight.

# III. THE MAGE --- THE WANDERING RUPTURE

The Mage did not survive intact.

The Mage became a migrating metaphysical rupture within the Aether
lattice.

## Rupture Behavior

-   Not fixed to one location
-   Attracted to structural stress
-   Migrates toward:
    -   High arcane residue
    -   Heavy Soul manipulation
    -   Institutional fracture
    -   Divine interference
    -   Elevated Crisis density

## Effects

-   Monster manifestations increase in region
-   Aether instability fluctuates
-   Magic volatility rises temporarily

The rupture never fully stabilizes. It drifts.

# IV. MONSTER ORIGIN CLARIFICATION

Monsters are manifestations of structural stress escaping through the
wandering rupture.

They are not inherently evil entities. They are pressure phenomena.

This aligns with Monster Doctrine without altering mechanical templates.
