# AETHERYN --- ECOLOGICAL CONTINUITY

Aetheryn is a self-regulating ecological system shaped by:

-   Climate bands
-   Migration corridors
-   Residue density
-   Institutional pressure
-   Keystone species balance

Every region contains:

1.  Primary Producers (flora)
2.  Pollinators & Decomposers (insects, fungi)
3.  Herbivores
4.  Mesopredators
5.  Apex Predators
6.  Scavengers
7.  Human Disruption Layer

No species exists without ecological function.

# INSECT & MICROFAUNA LAYER

## Pollinators

### Sunlace Bees (Eldrion / Sapphire)

Primary orchard pollinators. Dependent on hedgerow continuity. Collapse
triggers fruit failure and food pressure.

### Riverthread Moths (Veythar Corridor)

Night pollinators of reed systems. Residue-sensitive. Their absence
precedes fish spawning shifts.

### Glasswing Flies (Southern Sands)

Dawn pollinators of succulents. Short lifecycle tied to early morning
moisture cycles.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Decomposers

### Carrion Beetles (Continental)

Rapid carcass breakdown regulators. Population collapse increases
disease vectors.

### Scar Mites (Shattered Marches)

Residue-tolerant microfauna. Stabilize high-density soil zones.
